WHAT CAN YOU DO?

Switch to Firefox

The new Firefox is faster and has privacy protection features that blocks many advertisers and trackers by default. By the way it also works on your phone.

Give it a try

Search with the Duck

DuckDuckGo is a search engine that does not store any data or track you across websites while still providing useful results.

Meet Privacy Badger

Privacy Badger is a browser add-on that stops advertisers and other third-party trackers from secretly tracking where you go and what pages you look at on the web. If an advertiser seems to be tracking you across multiple websites without your permission, Privacy Badger automatically blocks that advertiser from loading any more content in your browser.
